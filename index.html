About Me

Sohel Hossain is an Embossed Designer and Automation Engineer specializing in industrial automation and embedded systems. He has hands-on experience working with ESP32, Arduino IDE, Arduino boards, STM32, and PIC microcontrollers. He focuses on designing efficient, scalable, and intelligent automation solutions for real-world industrial applications.

As the founder of Voltacdc, he is passionate about building innovative hardware and smart control systems that bridge the gap between creative embossing and industrial-grade reliability.
